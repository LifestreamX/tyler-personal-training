# 🎯 SEO Quick Reference - Content Writing Tips

## Writing Blog Posts / Content for Maximum SEO

### Include These Location Keywords Naturally:

- Boston, MA
- Greater Boston area
- Cambridge, Somerville, Brookline, Newton, Quincy
- Back Bay, South End, Downtown Boston, North End, Beacon Hill
- Massachusetts

### Example Good Titles:

✅ "5 Best Strength Training Exercises for Busy Boston Professionals"
✅ "How to Build Muscle at Home: A Guide for Greater Boston Residents"
✅ "Finding the Right Personal Trainer in Boston: What to Look For"
✅ "Online Personal Training vs In-Home Training in Boston: Which is Better?"

### Example Bad Titles:

❌ "5 Best Exercises" (too generic, no location)
❌ "Training Tips" (no specifics, no location)

## On-Page SEO Checklist for New Pages

When adding new pages or content:

1. **Title Tag** (H1)
   - Include primary keyword
   - Include location (Boston/area)
   - Keep under 60 characters
   - Example: "Online Personal Training - Boston & Greater Boston Area"

2. **Meta Description**
   - 150-160 characters
   - Include location and 1-2 keywords
   - Call-to-action at the end
   - Example: "Get personalized online personal training from NASM certified trainer in Boston, MA. Custom programs for strength, fat loss, and muscle building. Book free consultation today!"

3. **URL Structure**
   - Keep short and descriptive
   - Use hyphens, not underscores
   - Include keyword if natural
   - Good: `/boston-personal-training-services`
   - Bad: `/page123` or `/services_training_boston_ma_area`

4. **Content Structure**
   - H1: One per page, main keyword
   - H2: Section headers, include variations of keywords
   - H3: Subsections
   - Paragraphs: 2-4 sentences each
   - Use lists and bullets

5. **Internal Links**
   - Link to 2-3 other relevant pages
   - Use descriptive anchor text
   - Good: "learn more about [our training services](/services)"
   - Bad: "click [here](/services)"

6. **Images**
   - Always add alt text: "Tyler Allen personal trainer working with client in Boston"
   - Compress images (use WebP format)
   - Descriptive filename: `boston-personal-trainer-session.jpg`

## Keyword Density Guidelines

For a 500-word blog post:

- **Primary keyword** (e.g., "personal trainer Boston"): 3-5 times (1%)
- **Location mentions**: 5-10 times naturally
- **Related keywords**: 5-10 variations
- **DON'T stuff keywords** - write naturally for humans first

## Local SEO Pro Tips

### Always Mention:

1. Your service areas specifically
2. Landmarks near you or clients ("near Boston Common", "Back Bay area")
3. Local events when relevant
4. Neighborhood-specific content

### Examples:

✅ "I offer in-home personal training throughout Greater Boston, including Cambridge, Somerville, Brookline, Newton, and Quincy."
✅ "Whether you're a busy professional in the Financial District or a parent in Newton, I bring expert training to your home."
✅ "Perfect for Boston residents who want the convenience of training at home without a gym membership."

## SEO Don'ts

❌ **Don't:**

- Buy backlinks
- Keyword stuff
- Copy content from other sites
- Hide text/links
- Use spammy tactics
- Ignore mobile users
- Forget to update content
- Ignore negative reviews

✅ **Do:**

- Write for humans first, SEO second
- Be consistent with posting
- Focus on local keywords
- Get legitimate reviews
- Update old content regularly
- Build relationships locally
- Provide real value
