# Pricing & Policies — Tyler Allen Personal Training

This document outlines session rates, packages, travel policy, and cancellation terms. Use this as the single source of truth for website copy and booking.

## Standard Rates (USD)
- 30-minute session: $45 (intro offer: $30 for first session)
- 60-minute session: $80
- Remote (video) session: same as in-person unless otherwise noted

## Packages & Discounts
- 5-session pack (60-min): $380 (save 5%)
- 10-session pack (60-min): $760 (save 10%)
- Custom 12-week program: priced per plan (includes check-ins + program updates)

## Travel & Location
- In-person sessions within 5 miles: no travel fee.
- Beyond 5 miles: $0.75 per mile round-trip (or flat $10 travel fee for 5–10 miles).
- If travel becomes excessive, we will agree on a per-session travel fee before booking.

## Booking & Payment
- Book via the website booking page (Calendly). Payment methods: cash, card, or invoice (Stripe or payment processor).
- Full payment or a session credit card hold required to reserve the booking (configurable).
- Packages are prepaid and non-refundable but transferable within 6 months.

## Cancellation & No-Show Policy
- Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before the session for a full refund or reschedule.
- Cancellations within 24 hours: 50% charge of the session fee.
- No-shows: full session charge.

## Intro Offer & Trials
- First-time clients: discounted introductory session (30-min at $30) to assess goals and fit.

## Pricing Notes & Custom Work
- Pricing may vary for specialized services (rehab coaching, medical clearance, on-site corporate training).
- Long-term programs and corporate rates available — contact for custom quote.

## How to Display on Site
- Use a short table on the `Services` page and a clear CTA to `Booking`.
- Example table:

| Session | Length | Rate |
|---|---:|---:|
| Intro | 30 min | $30 |
| Single | 30 min | $45 |
| Single | 60 min | $80 |
| 10-pack | 10 × 60 min | $760 |

Include a short note about travel fees and the cancellation policy with the booking CTA.
