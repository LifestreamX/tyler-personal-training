# Pricing — Tyler Allen Personal Training

Simple, single-session pricing. Ask about multi-session packages by request.

## Rates (USD)

- 30-minute session: $45
- 60-minute session: $80

## Packages

Packages and discounts are available — please contact to inquire. (Not listed here to keep pricing simple.)

## Travel

- In-person sessions within 5 miles: no travel fee.
- Beyond 5 miles: $0.75 per mile (round-trip) unless a flat fee is agreed.

## Cancellation & No-Show

- Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before the session for no charge.
- Cancellations within 24 hours: 50% of the session fee.
- No-shows: full session charge.

## Booking

Book via the website booking page (Calendly). Payment details and policies are confirmed at booking.
